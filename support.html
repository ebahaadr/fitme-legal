Common questions

  • How do I create my avatar? Open the app, tap “Create Avatar”, and follow the steps with a clear photo of yourself.
  • How do credits work? AI try-ons use credits. New users get free credits to start, and you can get more with FitMe Pro or credit packs.
  • How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Go to your iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions, or App Store → Account → Subscriptions.
  • How do I restore my purchase? In the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • How do I delete my account? In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data.
